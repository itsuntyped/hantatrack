# HantaTrack

**Live site:** [hantatrack.net](https://hantatrack.net)

HantaTrack is a public, free-to-use platform for tracking the spread of **hantavirus** in near real time.

## Mission

Hantavirus outbreaks are rare, geographically scattered, and chronically underreported. When a cluster does emerge, the data is fragmented across health ministries, university research groups, news reports, and ad-hoc case logs — each in a different format, language, and update cadence. By the time a clear picture forms, the window for early public-health awareness has often closed.

**HantaTrack exists to close that gap.**

We aggregate hantavirus case data from multiple authoritative sources, normalize it into a single timeline, enrich it with AI-assisted analysis, and publish the result in near real time so that:

- **The public** can see what is happening, where, and how it is changing.
- **Journalists and clinicians** can get a fast, plain-language summary of the current situation without waiting for the next official bulletin.

Every data point on HantaTrack is traceable back to its source. We do not invent cases, and we clearly mark anything that is AI-generated commentary versus reported fact.

## What you get

- **Live map and timeline** — confirmed and suspected cases, plotted geographically and over time.
- **News ticker** — a marquee of the most recent hantavirus-related articles, pulled from Google News and refreshed hourly.
- **AI-powered analysis** — OpenAI-driven summaries that explain trends, highlight new clusters, and translate technical bulletins into plain language. Always labelled as AI output.
- **Source transparency** — every case carries a citation back to the originating report.

## Medical Disclaimer

> **This tracker is for informational and research purposes only.** Data may be incomplete, delayed, or inaccurate. Do not use this information to make medical decisions. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

HantaTrack aggregates publicly reported case data and is **not** affiliated with any health authority. AI-generated summaries on the site are clearly labelled and are commentary, not medical guidance.
